RetailPulse - Day 6 Summary

Sales Forecasting Using LSTM (PyTorch Lightning)

Dataset Used:
daily_sales.csv

Target Variable:
- Daily Sales

Preprocessing:
- MinMaxScaler
- Sequence Length = 30 Days
- Train/Test Split = 80/20

Model:
- LSTM
- Hidden Size = 50
- Adam Optimizer
- MSE Loss

Training:
- Epochs = 20

Outputs:
- lstm_forecast.csv
- lstm_forecast.png

Key Findings:
- LSTM successfully learned the overall sales trend.
- Predicted values closely follow actual sales patterns.
- Extreme sales spikes are smoothed by the model.
- Forecast can be improved using deeper architectures and hyperparameter tuning.
